Home Made Noodles


Ingredients
* Chicken broth (usually 2)
* Flour
* Eggs (usually 4)
* Salt and Pepper to taste
* 1 cup water per chicken broth
* 4 heaping tbsp butter per chicken broth
Directions
* Combine water, chicken broth and butter in large pot and set to boil
* While that warms combine four eggs with flour until doughy
* Spread flour on counter then place ball of dough down
* Roll flat with rolling pin then roll into a tube by hand
* Thinly slice and unravel noodles
* Once mixture boils add noodles stirring continuously
* Cook till desired texture (if still hard they’ll soften up in the crock pot)
