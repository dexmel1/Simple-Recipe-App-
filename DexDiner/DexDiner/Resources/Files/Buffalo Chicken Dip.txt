Buffalo Chicken Dip
(8 servings)
Ingredients
* 2 (8oz) packages cream cheese
* 1 cup ranch salad dressing
* ¾ cup Franks wing buffalo sauce
* 1 ½ cups shredded cheddat cheese
* 2 (10oz) cans Hormel chicken drained
* Tortilla chips
Directions
* Heat oven to 400°
* In large bowl, combine cream cheese, dressing, hot sauce and shredded cheese until well blended. Gently stir in chicken.
* Spoon dip into 2-quart casserole. Bake 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
